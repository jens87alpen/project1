What kind of beer do you like?

Ah, beer. What better to cool you off on a hot summer day? What better to warm you up after a day on the Colorado slopes? But with so many delicious brews out there, it can be hard to choose. Take this quiz to find out what beer fits your personality and maybe you'll have a new favorite

Ale

Ale is a type of beer brewed using a warm fermentation method, resulting in a sweet, full-bodied and fruity taste

Stout

Stout is a dark beer made using roasted malt or roasted barley, hops, water and yeast. Stouts were traditionally the generic term for the strongest or stoutest porters, typically 7% or 8% alcohol by volume (ABV), produced by a brewery.
